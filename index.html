projects

Here are the projects that I'm most proud of. This is an ever-growing portfolio. I'm working on a new project as we speak!

TRACER

TRACER is an app for cyclists and runners that allows users to build custom cycling and running routes and save them to their own accounts. Users can also log activities with or without their own built routes. Based on Strava's web app, and built with React/Redux on the front-end & Rails on the backend.

shapeShift

shapeShift is a fun and challenging, yet addicting typing game built with just JavaScript & HTML5 Canvas. Try your hand at the 'Challenge me' difficulty. Can you beat my high score of 100 words?

Yelp on Slack

Yelp on Slack is a Slack app dedicated to resolving team lunch indecision. The initiator provides some criteria, and the app returns a curated list of restaurants. Channel members react with an emoji to vote for their pick and the team goes to the winning option! Built with the MERN stack and the Slack and Yelp APIs.
